GL Africa is a non-profit organization, founded by a group of young people engaged in humanitarian aid to African developing countries. Our primary mission is aiding local children through the support provided by our European donors. We focus on providing orphans with quality education in order to help them achieve job opportunities afterwards. We do this in cooperation with the orphanage of Upendo and the Little Acorns elementary school.

Contact us

About us

The GL Africa sponsorship program is based on a form of continuous funding. We seek to provide children with secondary education so that they can further their education at university. These children will then go on to receive job offers from local GL Africa partners in the agricultural, health and IT sectors after graduating. In doing so, we strive to help bring up independent individuals who will become role models for other people.

Our partners

Upendo Children's Home

This home is an orphanage with a capacity for 60 children aged 0-6 years. Its staff includes external volunteers from Austria and Germany. Upendo was chosen because of its great organization, cleanliness and long-term experience and dedication to working with children.

Little Acorns School

This school is an outstanding elementary school and provides an extended curriculum of English. Some subjects are led by teachers from Europe. One of the biggest educational issues in Tanzania today is the low percentage of adequately trained teachers available, causing problems with the overall quality of the education imparted. Because of this, we have selected a school capable of diligently educating the children under our sponsorship to European standards.

Sponsorships

We are looking for sponsors for the children under our care, who need that support at the Upendo orphanage. These children will attend Little Acorns elementary school as a direct result of sponsorship donations. The sponsored children will then go on to study at secondary school after having finished elementary school. The sponsorship amount, however, remains the same as that during the child’s attendance of elementary school. The annual sponsorship donation is 25 000 CZK or 1000 USD. This amount covers the full cost of attending school, including annual school fees, uniforms, snacks, lunches, textbooks, exercise books, leisure activities and daily transport from the orphanage to school and back.
